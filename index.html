Why Mudrika?

Mudrika makes sign language learning simple and visual. Designed especially for beginners, it helps you learn Indian Sign Language step by step using images, videos, and interactive lessons. Instead of memorizing gestures, you understand them naturally through practice. Mudrika focuses on clarity, accessibility, and inclusivity — because communication should be easy for everyone.

“When words fail, hands begin to speak.”

Practice with Quizzes

Reinforce what you learn through quick, engaging quizzes.After each lesson, test your understanding with short quizzes that help you remember signs better.Instant feedback lets you know what you got right and where you can improve, making learning effective and enjoyable.

“Practice turns learning into confidence.”

Track Your Progress

See how far you’ve come, one sign at a time.Mudrika helps you track completed lessons and quiz scores, motivating you to stay consistent in your learning journey.By visualizing your progress, you stay encouraged to continue learning and achieve your goals at your own pace.

“Small steps today lead to big progress tomorrow.”
